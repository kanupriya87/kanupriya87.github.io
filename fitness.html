What is Zumba?

Zumba is a dance fitness program which involves dance and aerobic movements performed to energetic music. The exercises include music with fast and slow rhythms, as well as resistance training. The choreography incorporates hip-hop, soca, samba, salsa, merengue and mambo. Squats and lunges are also included.

Zumba is an incredible way to build cardiovascular strength, gain flexibility, and build muscle. It is also one of the most effective exercises that result in long sustaining and healthfully attained weight loss. But Zumba has many more life sustaining, health building, and mental strengthening qualities than just losing weight and looking great.

Bollywood Zumba

Bollywood-inspired dance-fitness program that combines dynamic choreography with the hottest music from around the world. Its 50-minute cardio workout cycles between higher and lower-intensity dance sequences to get you moving, sweating, and motivated all at the same time. We embody the infectious energy, expression and movement of Bollywood and aim to expand the reach of fitness to more people worldwide.

Because getting you in the best shape of your life is one thing, but giving you the platform to feel like a Bollywood star simultaneously is a whole other ball game.
